Structural analysis of steel pipe under certain stress and impact conditions, A Petroleum pipeline domain based project.

The project was carried out with the use of ANSYS(mechanical APDL) to analyse various challenges in petroleum pipeline domain. These challenges were translated to structural stress conditions and necessary analyses were carried out; comparison between dierent classes of pipe material under the same conditions was laid out to tackle such challenges.

Effect of Aspect ratio of Triangular Perforations in fins, on Natural Convective Heat Transfer Coefficient

An experimental and further ANSYS simulation was carried out to explore the effect of aspect ratio of triangular perforations in fins, on convective heat transfer coecient in natural convection ambient conditions.

Optimization and Fabrication of a double sided swing arm using sand binder jetting process

The project was part of the course Additive Manufacturing. In this project a double sided swing arm used in automotives such as motorcycles, ATVs was designed on solidworks and topologically optimised on Altair Inspire. Further, the model was a prototype of the scaled down versions of the model and the sand mold was 3D printed using PLA extrusion .

PART MANUFACTURING: Cast Steel Gate Valve

Design of cast steel gate valve on Solidworks. The specifications were based on assumed parameters (such as pressure, flow rate, etc.). 2D drawing sheets for individual comonents were further laid out with necessary GD&T callouts.
